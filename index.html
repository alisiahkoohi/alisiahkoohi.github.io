Research interests

I conduct research under the guidance of Maarten V. de Hoop and Richard G. Baraniuk that focuses on developing scalable deep learning methods to reliably solve computational problems in the physical and data sciences. This includes high-dimensional Bayesian inference for solving large-scale medical and geophysical inverse problems and unsupervised time series analysis with limited data.

Keywords: generative models, variational inference, inverse problems, uncertainty quantification, signal processing

I am a doctoral candidate in Computational Science and Engineering at Georgia Tech under the supervision of Felix Herrmann.

My multidisciplinary research revolves around developing generative-model-based methods to solve a variety of scientific computing problems in the fields of inverse problems, uncertainty quantification, and signal processing.

I will join Rice University's department of Computational & Applied Mathematics to work with Maarten de Hoop and Richard Baraniuk as a Simons Postdoctoral Fellow in Fall 2022.
